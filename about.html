What we do

We build software for manufacturing teams who are done duct-taping quality systems together with spreadsheets and email chains.

Two apps, one platform. Root Cause AI finds the problem. NConform proves the fix. Both are built around the standards your auditors actually care about — ISO 9001, IATF 16949, AS9100, and ISO 45001 — and priced so a single cell or line can start without sending a PO through three layers of approvals.

What we believe

Plain language wins

Operators and technicians shouldn't need a certification to log a defect. A 5 Why in plain English beats a perfectly formatted fishbone every time.

Standards-first, always

ISO 9001, IATF 16949, AS9100, ISO 45001. Audit trails, evidence capture, and revision control are built in, not bolted on after the first audit goes sideways.

Mobile-native, not mobile-adapted

Designed for the phone in your pocket, not a desktop app squeezed onto a tablet. Photos, voice notes, and signoffs happen where the work happens.

Made for makers

Quality managers, plant managers, maintenance leads, EHS teams — the people who run the floor, not the people who buy software in a boardroom.
