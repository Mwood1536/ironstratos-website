how it works

Three steps. Zero training needed.

Root Cause AI guides your team through structured investigations using plain conversation — no certifications, no manuals, no learning curve.

01

Tell it what happened

Open the app, start a new investigation, and describe the problem in your own words. No forms, no dropdowns — just talk.

02

AI asks the right questions

The AI walks you through a structured analysis — asking follow-ups, suggesting contributing factors, and helping you dig to the root cause.

03

Get your report

When you're done, Root Cause AI generates a complete investigation report with findings, contributing factors, and recommended actions.

features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

💬

Conversational RCA

AI-guided root cause analysis through natural conversation. No 5-why templates or fishbone diagrams required — just talk through the problem.

📋

5S Audits

Run standardized 5S workplace audits with guided checklists. Score, photograph, and track improvements over time.

📸

Photo Evidence

Attach photos directly to investigations and audits. Document conditions, defects, and improvements with timestamped visual evidence.

✅

Action Tracker

Assign corrective actions, set due dates, and track completion. Never lose an action item in a spreadsheet again.

📊

AI-Generated Reports

Get polished investigation reports generated automatically. Export and share with leadership, auditors, or your team.

📱

Built for Mobile

Designed to work where you work — on the floor, at the line, in the field. No desktop required.
