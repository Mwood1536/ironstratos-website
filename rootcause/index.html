features

Everything you need on the floor.

Built for technicians, supervisors, and quality engineers — not for desk work. Open the app, tell it what happened, get a structured investigation back.

💬

Conversational RCA

Describe the problem in your own words. The AI asks the right follow-up questions and walks you through the investigation — no templates, no training.

🧭

5 Why, Fishbone, Fault Tree

Switch between proven RCA methods on the same investigation. Use 5 Why for fast triage, Fishbone for cross-functional issues, Fault Tree for safety-critical events.

📊

AI-Generated Reports

Get a polished executive report when you finish — root cause, contributing factors, corrective actions. Export, share, and submit with one tap.

✅

Action Tracker

Assign corrective actions, set owners and due dates, and track completion. Open actions from every investigation in one place — no more spreadsheet chasing.

🔄

Turnover & Shift Logs

AI-assisted shift handover logs. Capture what happened, what's open, and what the next shift needs to know — searchable, sharable, audit-ready.

📸

Photo Evidence

Snap photos directly into investigations and procedures. Document failures, defects, and conditions with timestamped visual evidence.

📚

Procedure Library

Author SOPs and work instructions with AI assistance. Attach photos to each step, reference them during investigations, and keep your standards on the floor.

📱

Built for Mobile

Designed for the line, the field, the cell. Works offline-first — your data lives on your device, not in someone else's cloud.
