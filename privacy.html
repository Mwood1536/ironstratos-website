Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Iron Stratos LLC ("Iron Stratos," "we," "us," or "our") provides this Privacy Policy to explain how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information. IronStratos is the trade name of Iron Stratos LLC. This policy covers the ironstratos.com website and all of our products (collectively, the "Services"): Root Cause AI (root-cause-analysis mobile app), NConform (nonconformance/quality mobile app), and Pro Web (our web quality platform at app.ironstratos.com).

For the Root Cause AI app privacy policy, see here. The app-specific policy covers AI processing via Anthropic, Google AdMob ads on the free tier, and on-device data storage in detail.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use our Services, including:

  • Name, email address, organization details, and billing information when you create an account, subscribe, or contact us
  • Quality, operational, or other content you submit through our applications ("Customer Data"), including text descriptions and photographs
  • Device information, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage data, including feature usage, session duration, and interaction patterns

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, secure, and improve our Services; process subscriptions and payments; respond to your inquiries and provide support; communicate updates about our Services; analyze usage to improve user experience; and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information.

Data Storage and Security

Pro Web Customer Data is stored in Supabase, hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure in the United States (us-east-1 region). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures — including encryption in transit, access controls, and row-level data isolation between organizations — to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Third-Party Services and Sub-processors

We rely on the following third parties to operate our Services; each maintains its own privacy and security practices:

  • Anthropic — AI processing (analysis and generation features)
  • Supabase / Amazon Web Services (AWS) — Pro Web database, authentication, and data storage
  • Netlify — website and application hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing for Pro Web subscriptions
  • Google AdMob — advertising in the free tiers of our mobile apps
  • Apple App Store and Google Play — mobile app distribution and, where applicable, in-app purchases
  • Notion — used solely for our internal business operations; we do not store customer quality data in Notion

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Services. Following a verified deletion request (or account closure), we delete or anonymize your personal information and Customer Data within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it longer to comply with legal, tax, or accounting obligations, or to resolve disputes and enforce our agreements.

Your Privacy Rights (CCPA / GDPR)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, port, or delete your personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. California residents (CCPA/CPRA) have the right to know what we collect, to request deletion, and not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights; we do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under California law. Residents of the EEA/UK (GDPR) may exercise these rights and may lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. For Pro Web Customer Data, Iron Stratos LLC generally acts as a processor on behalf of your organization — see our Data Processing Addendum. To exercise any right, contact us at privacy@ironstratos.com.

Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for business use and are not directed to individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new policy on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
